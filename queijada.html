Queijadas de Laranja

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • Zest of 2 oranges
  • 1/4 cup fresh orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease a muffin tin or use paper liners.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:
    In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Combine Wet Ingredients:
    In another bowl, whisk together the melted butter, eggs, milk, fresh orange juice, orange zest, and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix Batter:
    Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined. Do not overmix.
  5. Fill Muffin Cups:
    Pour the batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 2/3 full.
  6. Bake:
    Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool and Serve:
    Allow the Queijadas de Laranja to cool in the muffin tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Serve at room temperature.
