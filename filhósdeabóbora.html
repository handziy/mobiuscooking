Filhós de Abóbora

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pumpkin puree
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Zest of 1 orange (optional)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Powdered sugar

Preparation

  1. Prepare the Pumpkin Puree:
    If you don't have pumpkin puree, cook and mash pumpkin until smooth.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:
    In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, and orange zest.
  3. Add Wet Ingredients:
    In a separate bowl, whisk together pumpkin puree, eggs, and milk.
  4. Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients:
    Pour wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Stir until just combined.
  5. Heat Oil for Frying:
    Heat vegetable oil to 350-375°F (175-190°C) in a deep pan or fryer.
  6. Forming the Filhós:
    Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil, forming irregular shapes.
  7. Fry Until Golden Brown:
    Fry filhós until golden brown on both sides (about 3-4 minutes per side). Fry in batches.
  8. Drain Excess Oil:
    Transfer filhós to a plate lined with paper towels to drain and cool slightly.
  9. Dust with Powdered Sugar:
    While warm, dust filhós with powdered sugar.
  10. Serve and Enjoy:
    Serve warm as a delightful snack or dessert.
