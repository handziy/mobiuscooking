Sonhos de Natal

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
    • <1i>1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Granulated sugar for dusting

Preparation

  1. Prepare Dough:
    In a saucepan, combine the butter, milk, salt, and granulated sugar. Heat over medium heat until the mixture comes to a boil. Remove from heat and add the flour all at once. Stir vigorously until a smooth dough forms.
  2. Add Eggs and Vanilla:
    Allow the dough to cool slightly, then add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the water and vanilla extract. Mix until the dough is well combined.
  3. Heat Oil:
    In a deep fryer or a large, deep pan, heat vegetable oil to 350°F (175°C).
  4. Form and Fry Dough Balls:
    Using two spoons, form small balls of dough and carefully drop them into the hot oil. Fry until golden brown, turning occasionally for even cooking.
  5. Drain and Dust:
    Remove the fried dough balls with a slotted spoon and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil. While still warm, roll the Sonhos in granulated sugar, ensuring they are well coated.
  6. Serve:
    Serve the Sonhos de Natal warm as a delightful and festive Christmas dessert in Vila do Conde!
