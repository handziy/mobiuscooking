About us


What is MobiusCooking?

MobiusCooking is your go-to cooking website located in Vila do Conde. Our team is passionate about all things cooking and are dedicated to providing you with the best recipes, tips and techniques to enhance your culinary skills.

MobiusCooking is not just a website; it's your digital kitchen companion, designed to elevate your culinary prowess and infuse excitement into your cooking routine. At the heart of our platform lies an extensive collection of meticulously curated recipes that cater to all tastes and skill levels. Whether you're a novice cook seeking beginner-friendly guidance or a seasoned chef hungry for innovative ideas, MobiusCooking is here to inspire and guide you on a flavorful journey.

More than a recipe repository, MobiusCooking is a dynamic culinary community where passionate food enthusiasts converge. Engage in discussions, share your culinary triumphs, and connect with like-minded individuals who share a common love for all things gastronomic. Rooted in the local flavors of Vila do Conde while drawing inspiration from global cuisines, MobiusCooking is your go-to destination for unlocking the joy of cooking, one delectable dish at a time. Join us, and let the kitchen adventures begin!


In simples terms we are:

  • Innovative: MobiusCooking constantly introduces fresh, cutting-edge culinary ideas and techniques to inspire creativity in the kitchen.
  • Inclusive: The platform caters to a diverse audience, offering recipes and tips suitable for all skill levels and dietary preferences.
  • Educational: The platform serves as an educational resource, providing not just recipes but also valuable tips, techniques, and information to enhance cooking proficiency.
  • Accessible: MobiusCooking is designed with user-friendly interfaces, making it easily accessible to individuals of all culinary backgrounds, from beginners to seasoned chefs.
  • Authentic: Grounded in the local flavors of Vila do Conde, MobiusCooking maintains authenticity while embracing a global perspective, offering a genuine culinary experience.


How and Where did we start?

In the bustling culinary landscape of Vila do Conde, MobiusCooking had its humble beginnings as a passion project among a close-knit group of friends. United by their love for cooking and an insatiable curiosity for flavors, the founders envisioned a platform that would transcend the traditional boundaries of a cooking website. In 2019, MobiusCooking emerged as a digital haven, poised to redefine the online culinary experience. Over the years, MobiusCooking has evolved into a thriving community and a go-to resource for culinary enthusiasts worldwide. From its early days of sharing local recipes and tips, the platform expanded its horizons, weaving together a rich tapestry of global influences. Today, MobiusCooking stands as a testament to the founders' dedication to fostering a space where food lovers, whether beginners or seasoned chefs, can come together to explore, learn, and celebrate the joy of cooking. As the site continues to grow, it remains rooted in its commitment to authenticity, innovation, and the shared love of all things gastronomic, inviting users to embark on an ever-expanding culinary adventure.


Connecting Locally: A Culinary Bridge in Vila do Conde

At MobiusCooking, our roots run deep in the local soil of Vila do Conde. We believe in the importance of community engagement and actively participate in local culinary events, workshops, and markets. By forging partnerships with local producers and artisans, we ensure that our recipes reflect the authentic flavors of our region. Our commitment extends beyond the digital realm as we strive to be a culinary bridge, connecting with the vibrant community of Vila do Conde and contributing to the flourishing tapestry of local gastronomy.

MobiusCooking is more than just a virtual space; it's a catalyst for local culinary appreciation. We organize community-centric initiatives, such as cooking classes and collaborative events, bringing people together to share in the joy of cooking. By engaging with our local community, we not only celebrate the richness of our culinary heritage but also contribute to the collective experience of exploring and savoring the diverse tastes of Vila do Conde. Join us in fostering a connection between the digital and the local, as we continue to build a culinary community that transcends borders and embraces the flavors of our home.
