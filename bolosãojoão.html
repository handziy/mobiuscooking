Bolo de São João de Vila do Conde

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • For the Cream Filling:
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons local liqueur (e.g., Ginjinha)
  • For Decoration:
  • Colorful sprinkles

Preparation

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease and flour two round cake pans.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients:
    In a bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Cream Butter and Sugar:
    In a separate large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add Eggs and Vanilla:
    Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  5. Incorporate Dry Ingredients:
    Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter and sugar mixture, alternating with the milk. Mix until just combined.
  6. Bake:
    Divide the batter between the prepared cake pans. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Prepare the Cream Filling:
    In a bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. Fold in the powdered sugar and local liqueur.
  8. Assemble the Cake:
    Once the cake layers are cooled, spread the cream filling between them.
  9. Decorate:
    Finish by decorating the top of the cake with colorful sprinkles.
  10. Serve:
    Slice and serve the Bolo de São João de Vila do Conde during the festive São João celebrations!
