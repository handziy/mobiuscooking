Bolo de Noz e Mel

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. Preheat the Oven:
    Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. Cream Butter and Honey:
    In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and honey until light and fluffy.
  3. Add Eggs and Vanilla:
    Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Mix Dry Ingredients:
    In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and a pinch of salt.
  5. Incorporate Dry Ingredients:
    Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter and honey mixture, alternating with the plain yogurt. Mix until just combined.
  6. Fold in Walnuts:
    Gently fold in the finely chopped walnuts into the batter.
  7. Bake:
    Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan. Bake in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool and Serve:
    Allow the Bolo de Noz e Mel to cool in the pan for a few minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely. Slice and serve at room temperature.
